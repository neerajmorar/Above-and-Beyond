The Food

The food we serve here consists of a range of spices meaning the majority our meals are mildly hot, unless otherwise stated. In addition, the food here may contain nuts, bones, gluten, lactose, and other allergens so do please inform the staff when ordering for your benefit.

Further down the page you will find a table consisting of set meals and information about when you can order these meals.

Set Meals

Set Meals are available at lunchtime for you to enjoy at an affordable price. These are also available for any events organised.

They work by you picking two starters from one of the two menus, one main from Menu A and one main from Menu B, and then two desserts from one of the two menus. All sides are available for both menus.

Set Meals are priced at £18.00 per head, excluding sides.
