Events

It is also possible for us to hold and hire out parts of our restaurant for any events you may want to organise, whether they are birthday parties or business lunches. We also have a dance floor located upstairs in tandem with another bar for you to use that can hold up to 250 people. DJ's will have to be provided by yourself.

The restaurant closes at 1:00am in order to comply with the local authorities in order to reduce noise pollution and disruption to local residents. We are also not responsible for any belongings that may get damaged, lost, or stolen during any held event. We are also diligent on security and we expect you to comply with our rules in order to make other guests dining here happy.
